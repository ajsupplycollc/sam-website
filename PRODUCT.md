# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Small-business owners (contractors, shops, service businesses) arriving from Jereme's TikTok/IG/LinkedIn videos — mostly on phones, curious about "an AI that runs your business," deciding whether to book a free 15-minute call. Not technical; they buy outcomes, not stacks.

## Product Purpose

SAM's own funnel page: convert video traffic into booked calls. Tiers: Basic $500 / Business $1K + $299mo / Curated $1.5K + $499mo / Website $500. Everything routes to strangeadvancedmarketing.com (canonical — never strangeonekenobi.com).

## Brand Personality

"Thoughtfully curated." Operator, not agency — SAM deploys a system the client owns (local install, no lock-in). Confident, radically transparent (WHY → HOW), working proof over promises. Design identity: **Operator Console** — the site should feel like the product: a live, capable console.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing grammar (gradient text, glass cards everywhere, hero-metric template).
- Agency-speak ("we scale brands"). SAM is not an agency.
- GREEN as a brand color — green belongs to clients (e.g. First Class Auto Glass). SAM accent = electric blue #00d4ff on navy.

## Design Principles

1. The site IS the demo — showing the console working beats describing it.
2. Navy (#2d3748 family, dark console blacks) + one electric-blue accent (#00d4ff). One accent family, total discipline.
3. Voice-to-execution is the product story: you talk, it runs your business.
4. Real proof (real videos, real numbers, real client outcomes) over stock anything.
5. Fast on a phone from a TikTok link — the funnel starts mid-scroll, hooked or gone in seconds.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on dark surfaces; reduced-motion fallbacks for all scroll/entrance motion; tap targets ≥44px; booking CTA reachable without precision.
