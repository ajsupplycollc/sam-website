Questions

The honest answers.

What's Telegram?

A free messaging app, like WhatsApp. Two minutes to download, and it's the only thing you'll ever need. Your agent lives there as a private contact only you can message.

Do I need to set anything up?

No. You fill out the questionnaire, we build and host everything. Connecting your email and calendar is a single link we send you: tap, sign in to Google, allow. Done from your phone.

What if I don't use Gmail?

Tell us your provider in the questionnaire. Google connects with one tap; Yahoo, Outlook and others are wired by us during your setup window, before your agent goes live.

Is my business data safe?

Your agent has its own virtual computer: a sealed, private workspace in the cloud. Its memory belongs to you alone, encrypted, isolated from every other client, and never used to train anything.

Is this a chatbot for my customers?

No — it's yours. A private operator for the owner. It works for you, knows your business, and answers to nobody else.

What if it gets something wrong?

Tell it. "Actually, driveway cleaning starts at $150." Corrected, saved, and remembered from then on. Everything in its memory can be changed just by asking. That's the whole interface.

What if I want it to do more?

It grows with you. Extra connections and bigger capabilities are added case by case. Ask your agent, it will tell us.

Can I cancel?

Anytime. Month to month, no contract.
