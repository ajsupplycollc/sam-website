Service providers

We use a small number of trusted providers to run our business, including form processing (Web3Forms), email (Google Workspace), and website hosting (GitHub Pages). These providers process data only as needed to deliver their service.

Client project data

For sovereign and enterprise deployments, client data remains within the client's own environment, is not used to train any model, and is subject to the security terms of that engagement.

We retain inquiry information for as long as needed to respond and to maintain our business records, then delete it when no longer required. We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your choices
